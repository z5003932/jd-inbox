## What I do

I work across the full arc — strategy, discovery, design, and delivery. I own the full product loop: figure out what to build, why and how, then ship and measure it.

What I keep seeing: products failing — AI and non-AI — not because the technology isn't ready, but because the fundamentals weren't done. Nobody talked to the humans doing the actual workflow before automating it. The UX assumed the model would behave consistently. Nobody defined what good output actually looks like before shipping. Not technical failures — product failures, BA failures, service design failures. Almost always preventable.

The last two years I've added something most senior practitioners haven't: I actually build AI systems. Not "use AI tools" — build production pipelines. Claude API, human-in-the-loop architectures, LLM evaluation frameworks. Production Python.
